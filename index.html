The gears you need

Clothes



The right clothing are absolut critical for a safe sea kayaking experiance. Selection of clothes need to be adapted to weather conditions, water temperature, air temperature, wind to name a few. As well where you will be kayaking, calm water in protected lake or open ocean. To dress in layers combining breathing out body heat and in the same time protect from wind and water is necessary. We will as well guide you through how to think when selecting a lifejacket.

  • lifejacket - allowing free movement in the kayak
  • underlay in syntetic material which is not absorbing water
  • outher shell protecting from wind and water
  • when submiting to course you will have a detailed instruction
