Kayaking

During the course we will guide you through what gears are needed and why
Different kayaks, paddles and PFDs will be avilable to test

  • Safety

    Be safe on the water with right equpment like personal floting device (PFD), bilige pump, whistle, float bag and more

  • Kayak

    Chose a kayak matching your skills. We will discuss kayak design and stability, different parts of the kayak and how to use them. You will be able to try different kayaks during the course.

  • Paddle

    Different paddles for different purpose. We will discuss design and size of blades, diffrent shafts and how to hold the paddle. You will be able to test differnt paddles during the course.

  • Clothes

    An essential part of the experiance is to use right clothes. They need to protect you from wind, water, cold and in the same time be able to breath out the body heat. We will share our set-up for diffrent conditions.

Technique

It's all about the technique
Our objective is to make you a better and safer paddler

  • Know the paddle

    Get used to the paddle, how to hold it and use it to balance and lean on to increase the stability.

  • Basic Stroke

    Are there more than forward stroke? The anser is yes, it will be situations when you need reverse, sweep and draw the kayak and we will parctice these four basic stokes during the course.

  • Advanced technique

    We will practice advanced stoke technique to controll the kayak and to effeciently be abel to go longer distances. For those who want we will parctice rolling technique.
