Kayaking

The gears you need

Clothes



The right clothing are absolut critical for a safe sea kayaking experiance. Selection of clothes need to be adapted to weather conditions, water temperature, air temperature, wind to name a few. As well where you will be kayaking, calm water in protected lake or open ocean. To dress in layers combining breathing out body heat and in the same time protect from wind and water is necessary. We will as well guide you through how to think when selecting a lifejacket.

Some of the topic that will be discussed:


  • lifejacket that allows free movement in the kayak
  • underlay in synthetic material which is not absorbing water
  • outer shell protecting from wind, water and cold
  • when submitting to course you will have a detailed instruction

Paddle



The right paddle is important to smoothly drive the kayak through the water and to avoid unnecessary tension in muscles. During the course we will explain different paddles and how these differ with regard to design and paddle technique. There will be different paddles to test.

We will have guide you in:


  • right size with regards to area of the blade
  • right size with regards to length of the shaft
  • how to hold the paddle
  • different paddles will be available during the course

Kayak



The right kayak will make the difference if your first trip will be the only one or if it will be the start of a lifelong passionated journy. During the cource we will explain different kayak models, their design and what important to think of. There will be different kayaks to test.

We will guide and expain follwoing:


  • kayak disign and stability
  • kayak design and how to go where you want
  • how to act when you fall into the water
  • different kayaks will be available during cource to test
